I am a creative technologist, acoustic engineer, and audiovisual systems designer, with a focus on immersive sound and generative media.

I help designers and artists realize digital and physical installations in the built environment on a variety of scales, from small temporary sculptures to large multimedia entertainment venues. I also create my own media artwork.

I've lived, studied, and worked in France, the Netherlands, and the United States.
