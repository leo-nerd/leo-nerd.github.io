I am a senior experience designer and multi-media technologist.

I help designers, artists, and visionaries like you tell meaningful stories in the built environment using experiential tools and technology, from small self-contained pieces to large-scale integrated narrative environments and multi-media systems. I care deeply about how users engage with physical space and believe that carefully coordinated design can achieve sustainable, rewarding, and heightened experiences for everyone.

I've lived, studied, and worked in France, the Netherlands, and the United States. I have over ten years' experience as a designer and engineer for the built environment, delivering a wide range of experiential and cultural projects from concept to implementation.

I am a founding member of Collective Scope, a mixed-media artist group. We develop site-specific multi-media installations aimed at challenging our incumbent relationships with more-than-human agents in urban environments.

I'd love to hear about your new project.
