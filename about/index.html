I am a creative technologist and multi-media experience designer.

I help designers and artists achieve their vision and tell their story using experiential tools and technology. My work ranges from small self-contained pieces to large-scale complex integrated multimedia systems. I also create my own mixed-media artwork.

I've lived, studied, and worked in France, the Netherlands, and the United States. I have over ten years' experience as a designer and engineer for the built environment, managing and delivering a variety of physical and digital projects from concept to implementation.
